The AuxilioOS project is an Operating System like no other. It's light-weight and easy to use. Perfect for people of all ages to use! AuxilioOS is predownloaded with tons of programming content so that people can dive into something they are interested in!

Our mission:
Expose programming to low-income or poor-performing children to programming and to provide a programming medium for children to explore.

Future Goals: -To donate computers (raspberry pis) with our operating system to poor-performing or under-served communities in the US -To donate computers with our operating system internationally.
